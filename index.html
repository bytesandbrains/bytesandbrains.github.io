Full-stack Development

We provide amazing backend, web, and mobile app development services. We work hand in hand with your development team to integrate development and QA processes into your team's daily workflow. Our knowledge across languages, technologies and platforms makes us the ideal partner your next outsourced project.

M&A Technical Due Diligence

Our broad set of skills ensure that can review all aspects of the product including algoritm performance, software development, licensing, team skills, integration with hospital systems (PACS, HL7, EMR), patient privacy, and regulatory compliance such as GDPR and technical documentation (pre- and post CE, ISO13485 etc).
